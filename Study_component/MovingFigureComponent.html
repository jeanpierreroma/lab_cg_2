What is affine transformation?

It is a transformation between two spaces of the same dimension and which is linear

Properties of affine transformation

- an n-dimensional object is mapped to an n-dimensional object, a point to a point.
- the parallelism of lines and planes is preserved.
- the proportions of parallel objects are preserved (lengths of segments on parallel lines and areas on parallel planes).

Affine transformations:

-displacement/displacement
-scaling (increase/decrease)
-turning the corner
